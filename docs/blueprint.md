# **App Name**: RETREAT

## Core Features:

- User Role Identification: Identify if the user is a host, seeker, vendor or space owner, guiding them to the appropriate user experience.
- Retreat Listing: Hosts can upload images, descriptions, and reviews of their retreats. Hosts are enabled to connect with vendors.
- Vendor & Space Listing: Vendors and space owners can create profiles showcasing their services, pricing, reviews, and availability.
- Retreat Search & Filtering: Seekers can filter retreats by type, cost, location, and other criteria to find suitable options.
- Secure Payment Processing: Facilitate secure payments between seekers, hosts, and vendors with automated commission calculation.
- Commission Enforcement Tool: AI tool monitors bookings and communications to detect and prevent attempts to circumvent the app's commission system.
- Review & Rating System: Enable users to leave reviews and ratings for retreats, vendors, and spaces.

## Style Guidelines:

- Colors: Black, white, beige (light tan).
- Highlight color: bright orange/ red "poppy color".
- Font: Elegant and modern.
- Lots of white spaces, clean lines, upscale and trendy.
- No animations, only places for me to add images.
